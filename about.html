Who Are We

Philanthropist Cats

Hobbes and PB

Two brothers with tired paws embarked on a journey to bring biscuits to the world while combating the feline unemployement rate. Cabiscuits started in a small kitchen in a town house in Chapel Hill, NC. The demand for biscuits increased and we had to create a store and employ many other biscuit making cats! Our brand has gotten so big we know need a website to help manage large online orders.
